# 📊 Automated CRM Sales KPI Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project delivers a fully structured **Sales KPI and Operational Performance Dashboard** designed for a multi-brand home services business.

The dashboard replicates a real-world business scenario where CRM data (SmartMoving-style) is transformed into **daily, weekly, and monthly performance reporting** to support sales and operations teams.

The solution focuses on tracking:
- Sales performance  
- Lead responsiveness  
- Conversion efficiency  
- Operational data quality  

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A growing home services company operating multiple brands needed a centralized reporting system to:

- Monitor how quickly leads are contacted  
- Track conversion from leads to booked jobs  
- Evaluate sales team performance  
- Measure operational data quality passed to fulfillment teams  
- Generate consistent daily, weekly, and monthly reports  

The existing process lacked visibility into:
- Missed or delayed follow-ups  
- Conversion inefficiencies  
- Inconsistent sales performance  
- Data quality issues impacting operations  

---

## 🧩 Solution

A **Power BI dashboard system** was developed to transform CRM data into actionable insights.

The solution includes:

### 🔹 Daily Report (Operational Monitoring)
- Leads Today  
- Jobs Booked Today  
- Revenue Today  
- Conversion Rate (vs previous day)  
- Lead responsiveness indicators  

### 🔹 Weekly Summary (Performance Tracking)
- Weekly Leads, Jobs, Revenue  
- Week-over-Week (WoW) comparison  
- Sales rep performance vs activity  

### 🔹 Monthly Dashboard (Management View)
- Total Revenue: **$2.26M**  
- Total Leads: **3K**  
- Conversion Rate: **25.47%**  
- Revenue per Lead: **$753.70**  
- Revenue trends by month  
- Performance breakdown by salesperson and brand  

### 🔹 Operations & Data Quality Tracking
- Quote Close Rate (**~34–36%**)  
- Quote Accuracy  
- Address Completion (**100%**)  
- Crew Notes Completion (**~47%**)  

---

## 📊 KPIs Implemented

### Responsiveness
- Time to First Contact  
- Lead Follow-up Time  
- No-Contact Rate (**~59.1%**)  

### Sales Performance
- Revenue by Salesperson  
- Conversion Rate (Leads → Jobs)  
- Revenue per Lead (**$753.70**)  

### Brand Performance
- Revenue by Brand:
  - Eco Movers  
  - Island Movers  
  - Piano Movers  
  - Sea to Sky Moving  

### Execution Quality
- Follow-up Completion  
- Quote Accuracy  
- Address Completion  
- Crew Notes Completion  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Revenue reached **$2.26M**, driven by improved conversion efficiency  
- Daily revenue increased by **+20.4%**, weekly revenue by **+58.2%**  
- Daily leads declined by **-30%**, indicating reduced inflow  
- Overall conversion rate is **25.47%**, with short-term improvements observed  
- Response time is high (**~24.78 hours**), delaying engagement  
- No-contact rate is elevated (**~59.1%**), leading to lost opportunities  
- Sales performance is uneven, with top performers driving most revenue  
- Operational quality is inconsistent, particularly in **crew notes completion (~47%)**  

---

## 📊 Business Impact

- Identified gaps in lead responsiveness affecting conversion  
- Highlighted inefficiencies in sales execution across team members  
- Exposed operational data quality issues impacting service delivery  
- Provided a centralized reporting system for decision-making  

---

## 🧾 Conclusion

The business shows strong revenue performance and improving conversion efficiency, but is constrained by declining lead volume, slow response times, and inconsistent operational processes.

Addressing responsiveness and process standardization will significantly improve overall performance.

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Reduce response time from **~24.78 hours to under 4 hours**  
- Implement structured follow-up workflows to reduce **~59.1% no-contact rate**  
- Improve lead generation to offset **-30% daily decline**  
- Standardize sales processes using top performer strategies  
- Increase crew notes completion beyond **47%**  
- Improve quote accuracy and operational consistency
